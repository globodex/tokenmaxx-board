## Style Prompt

Restrained OpenAI-adjacent product demo for Tokenmaxx Board. The teaser should feel like a calm product walkthrough, not an ad: off-white surfaces, black hero bands, quiet borders, tabular stats, minimal copy, and smooth focus-pull transitions. The product UI is the visual hero.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#f6f6f1`
- Ink: `#080807`
- Muted text: `#6f6d66`
- Soft border: `#dfddd3`
- Panel: `#fffffb`
- Dark panel: `#050505`
- Gold rank: `#f4bd4d`
- Blue data accent: `#0872ce`

## Typography

- Primary: renderer-safe `sans-serif`
- Data/code: `IBM Plex Mono`

## Motion

- Calm focus-pull and blur crossfade transitions.
- Product panels enter with small y-offset, opacity, and scale.
- Metric rows and chips stagger in subtly.

## What NOT to Do

- No neon, purple/blue gradients, or decorative blobs.
- No hype copy, huge marketing cards, or fake OpenAI branding.
- No jump cuts.
- No invented product surfaces beyond what the site already does.
